Bogosort

Sorting that Rocks

Bogosort is a sorting algorithm. It is also known by other names, including monkey sort, and shotgun sort. It is not the fastest, in fact it can probably considered the slowest as everytime you try the Bogosort your elements may potentialy never sort.

The algorithm for this sort starts with an array of elements. Check the array to see if it is sorted or not. If not, randomize the entire array and check again.

*****Bewarned that arrays greater than 6 or 7 may take a very long time*****
